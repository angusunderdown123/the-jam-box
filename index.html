About Us

The Jam Box is a family-owned business that specializes in handmade jams and preserves. Our products are made with only the freshest and highest quality ingredients, and we take pride in our commitment to supporting local farmers and growers.

Our team has over 20 years of experience in the food industry, and we are passionate about creating delicious and unique flavor combinations that are sure to satisfy your taste buds.
