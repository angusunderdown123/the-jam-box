Features of Our Shop

The shop is modern with a light farmy feel, the black paint and wood are contrasted by bright labels that stand out only below the jam. There is storage along one wall and boxes of JOTM on the other wall. On the shorter counter in front of the boxes is a glass case which shows off the beautiful jam cakes. There are many displays of jam and preserved fruits.
